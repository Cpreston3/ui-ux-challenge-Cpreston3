User story

As an Amazon user I would like access to international products for my business at a lower cost

Behaviors

John drinks coffee on a regular basis. John wakes up 2 hours before he goes to his coffee shop and drinks coffee on his patio every morning before work. John likes to read the New York Times while drinking coffee in the morning.

Attitude

John likes to shop efficiently, He commutes to work via cycling evey day. He likes to try new things to keep his business relevant. He also supports small business like himself.

situation

John would like easy access to his products for his small coffee shop. He wants to be able to get things form all over the world at a great price. John is willing to try new coffees that will give his shop an edge or signature. He is part of "Amazon Business".

goals

  • support his family
  • get products fast at a good value
  • pass down his values
